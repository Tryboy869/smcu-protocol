# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

SMCU is an open protocol built for the benefit of all AI systems and their users.
We maintain a welcoming, respectful, and technically rigorous community.

**Expected behavior:**
- Critique ideas, not people
- Back disagreements with technical arguments
- Give credit where it is due
- Keep discussions focused on the protocol

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Personal attacks or harassment
- Submitting intentionally false or harmful registry entries
- Coordinating validator votes in bad faith
- Claiming false authorship of contributions

## Enforcement

Violations may result in: warning → temporary ban → permanent ban from the repository.

Reports: `anzize.contact@proton.me`

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces (GitHub, discussions, registry).
